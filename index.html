The Artist

Dhyan Rajamani

Dhyan, or Dhyan Rajamani, is a 21-year-old artist from Maryland who loves music. Now currently a songwriter and part of the thriving Pittsburgh music scene, Dhyan makes music that ranges from fun acoustic long songs to hype hip hop/RnB. It wouldn't be uncommon to hear the release of an indie-pop song after putting out a song on a Latin-trap beat. A true student of the craft, Dhyan does his best to make sure you "hear his smile".

Dhyan is heavily inspired by the likes of Bruno Mars, Ed Sheeran, Kanye West, Amy Winehouse, Chance the Rapper, and many more pop powerhouses. A relatable and fun goofball who can also speak to the heart when needed.
